76. Wally and the Clever Raccoons
Scene 1: Wally sees Riley the Raccoon fiddling with a log. "What are you doing, Riley?" he asks.
Scene 2: "I’m looking for bugs to eat!" Riley says, lifting the log to reveal squirming bugs.
Scene 3: Wally watches as Riley carefully selects his snack. "You’re good at this!" Wally says.
Scene 4: Riley explains, "I use my hands to feel around for food." Wally tries, mimicking Riley’s movements.
Scene 5: "I found one!" Wally says excitedly. Riley laughs. "You’re a quick learner, Wally!"
