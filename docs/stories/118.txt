219. Wally and the Ring of Saturn
Scene 1: Wally’s rocket flies toward Saturn. "Look at those rings!" he gasps.
Scene 2: Sera the Space Bird swoops by. "Saturn’s rings are made of ice and rocks."
Scene 3: Sera flies near the rings. "Want to glide with me?"
Scene 4: Wally tries flying but slides on the icy surface. "It’s slippery!"
Scene 5: "Saturn is beautiful!" Wally says, floating gently above the rings.
