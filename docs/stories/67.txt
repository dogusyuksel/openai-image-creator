139. Wally and the Baboons' Picnic
Scene 1: Wally sees baboons sharing fruit under a tree. "Can I join your picnic?" he asks.
Scene 2: Benny the Baboon hands him a banana. "We love eating together!" Benny says.
Scene 3: Wally shares berries he found nearby. "Sharing makes it more fun!" he says.
Scene 4: The baboons play nearby, tumbling around the grass.
Scene 5: "Join us anytime for lunch!" Benny says, as Wally waves goodbye.
