200. Wally and the Sleeping Stegosaurus
Scene 1: Wally sees a dinosaur with plates on its back. "What are those for?"
Scene 2: Stella the Stegosaurus yawns. "They help keep me cool or warm."
Scene 3: Wally touches one plate gently. "It feels strong!"
Scene 4: Stella chuckles. "They also protect me from other dinosaurs."
Scene 5: "Your armor is awesome!" Wally says as Stella falls back asleep.
