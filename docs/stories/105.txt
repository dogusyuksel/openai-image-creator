391. Wally and the Snowy Night
Scene 1: Wally peeks outside. "It’s snowing!"
Scene 2: Mama Wolf wraps him in a blanket. "The snow is tucking the world in for the night."
Scene 3: Wally imagines snowflakes gently covering the forest. "Like a soft, white blanket."
Scene 4: The snow falls slowly, and Wally’s eyes get heavy.
Scene 5: "Goodnight, snow," Wally whispers as he falls asleep.
