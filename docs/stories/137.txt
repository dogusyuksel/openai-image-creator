380. Wally and the Whispering Wind
Scene 1: Wally lies in bed, listening to the wind outside. "It sounds like it's talking!"
Scene 2: Mama Wolf smiles. "The wind whispers bedtime stories to the trees."
Scene 3: Wally closes his eyes. "I can hear it telling the leaves goodnight."
Scene 4: The wind gently sways the branches outside. "Goodnight, trees," Wally yawns.
Scene 5: Wally falls asleep, dreaming of gentle wind songs.
