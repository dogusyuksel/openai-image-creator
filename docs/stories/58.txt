223. Wally and the Friendly Comet
Scene 1: A comet zooms by. "Wait for me!" Wally calls.
Scene 2: Connie the Comet slows down. "I’m heading past Earth!"
Scene 3: Wally grabs onto her tail. "This is so fast!"
Scene 4: Connie explains. "Comets are made of ice and dust. We glow when near the Sun."
Scene 5: "I’ll look for you in the night sky!" Wally says as Connie waves goodbye.
