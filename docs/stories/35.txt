181. Wally and the Polar Bear’s Ice Slide
Scene 1: Wally steps onto slippery ice. "Whoa! I can’t stand up!" he yelps.
Scene 2: Paula the Polar Bear slides past. "Sliding is fun! Try it with me!" she says.
Scene 3: Wally carefully sits down. Paula gives him a push, and he glides across the ice.
Scene 4: "Wheee!" Wally laughs. Paula teaches him how to steer by leaning.
Scene 5: "Sliding is the best part of winter!" Wally says as they slide together.
