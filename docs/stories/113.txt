210. Wally and the Spinosaurus Fishing Trip
Scene 1: Wally walks near a river and spots a large dinosaur with a sail on its back. "What are you doing?"
Scene 2: Spencer the Spinosaurus wades into the water. "I’m fishing!"
Scene 3: Spencer uses his claws to catch fish. "I love water!" he says.
Scene 4: Wally watches in awe. "I didn’t know dinosaurs fished!"
Scene 5: "Come back anytime to fish with me!" Spencer says as Wally waves.
