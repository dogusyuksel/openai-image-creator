237. Wally and the Lion’s Roar
Scene 1: Wally hears a loud roar. "That’s the loudest sound ever!"
Scene 2: Leo the Lion approaches. "My roar warns others to stay away."
Scene 3: Wally tries to roar back but only howls softly. "I need practice!"
Scene 4: Leo chuckles. "Your howl will grow stronger, just like my roar."
Scene 5: "I’ll keep trying!" Wally says as Leo’s roar echoes across the savannah.
