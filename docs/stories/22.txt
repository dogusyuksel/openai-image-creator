118. Wally and the Tapir's Mud Bath
Scene 1: Wally hears splashing in a muddy pond. "Who’s having fun over there?" he asks.
Scene 2: Terry the Tapir rolls in the mud. "Mud keeps me cool!" Terry explains.
Scene 3: Wally jumps into the mud too. "It’s squishy!" he giggles.
Scene 4: Terry and Wally splash and roll around, getting completely covered in mud.
Scene 5: "I feel so cool now!" Wally laughs as they rinse off in the stream nearby.
