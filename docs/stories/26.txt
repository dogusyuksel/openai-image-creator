187. Wally and the Iceberg Adventure
Scene 1: Wally sees a floating iceberg. "It’s like a floating mountain!" he says.
Scene 2: Paddy the Penguin waddles up. "Hop on! Icebergs carry us places."
Scene 3: Wally steps carefully onto the iceberg. "It’s cold but fun!"
Scene 4: Paddy shows Wally how to balance. "Keep low to avoid slipping!"
Scene 5: "Floating on ice is the best!" Wally says as they ride across the water.
