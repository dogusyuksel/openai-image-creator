198. Wally and the Flying Pterodactyl
Scene 1: Wally spots shadows overhead. "Are those birds?" he wonders.
Scene 2: Patty the Pterodactyl glides down. "Nope! We’re flying reptiles."
Scene 3: Patty spreads her wings. "I fly over the sea to catch fish!"
Scene 4: Wally watches Patty dive, returning with a fish. "That’s amazing!" he says.
Scene 5: "Next time, I’ll take you flying!" Patty laughs as she soars into the sky.
