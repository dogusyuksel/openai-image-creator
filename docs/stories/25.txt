77. Wally and the Frogs at the Lily Pond
Scene 1: Wally sits by a pond, hearing croaks. "Who’s singing?" he wonders.
Scene 2: Freddy the Frog leaps onto a lily pad. "We love to sing at the pond!" Freddy explains.
Scene 3: Wally watches as frogs hop from pad to pad. "Can I try?" he asks.
Scene 4: Wally carefully steps onto a stone, pretending to be a frog. "Ribbit!" he croaks.
Scene 5: "You’re one of us now, Wally!" Freddy laughs. Wally smiles. "I love playing with you frogs!"
