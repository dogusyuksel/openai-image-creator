115. Wally and the Busy Ant-Eater
Scene 1: Wally hears sniffing near the ground. "What are you looking for?" he asks.
Scene 2: Andy the Anteater sniffs around. "I’m finding ants for lunch!" Andy explains.
Scene 3: Wally follows Andy as he uses his long tongue to gather ants. "You eat so many!" Wally says in surprise.
Scene 4: Andy laughs. "Ants are my favorite snack!" Wally tries to count the ants, but there are too many.
Scene 5: "You’re a great ant-finder, Andy!" Wally says, waving as Andy continues his search.
