158. Wally and the Thunderstorm
Scene 1: Dark clouds form as Wally climbs higher. "Uh-oh, it’s going to rain!" he says.
Scene 2: Toby the Tortoise hides under a rock. "Storms are safer here!" he tells Wally.
Scene 3: Wally snuggles under the rock with Toby. "I like this cozy spot!"
Scene 4: As the rain clears, a rainbow appears. "Look at that!" Wally gasps.
Scene 5: "Storms aren’t so bad with friends!" Wally says as they admire the rainbow.
