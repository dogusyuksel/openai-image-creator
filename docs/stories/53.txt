383. Wally and the Sleepy River
Scene 1: "What’s that sound outside?" Wally asks, hearing water trickling.
Scene 2: Mama Wolf explains. "The river sings lullabies to the forest."
Scene 3: Wally listens closely. "It’s like a gentle song."
Scene 4: The soft flow makes his eyelids heavy. "I’m getting sleepy too."
Scene 5: Wally falls asleep, carried away by the sound of the river.
