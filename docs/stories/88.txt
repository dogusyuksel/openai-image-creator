190. Wally and the Arctic Hare’s Leap
Scene 1: Wally sees tracks in the snow. "Who made these giant leaps?"
Scene 2: Archie the Arctic Hare hops up. "I jump high to escape danger!"
Scene 3: Wally tries hopping but lands with a thud. "You make it look easy!"
Scene 4: Archie grins. "Strong legs help me jump far."
Scene 5: "Let’s keep hopping!" Wally says as they leap across the snow together.

# Dinosaur and Ancient Animal Adventures (Stories 196-215)
Wally the Baby Wolf travels back in time to explore the prehistoric world. He meets friendly dinosaurs and ancient creatures, learning about their unique traits and habits. These stories focus on curiosity, discovery, and the wonders of ancient life.
