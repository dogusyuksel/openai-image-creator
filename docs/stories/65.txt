71. Wally and the Busy Ants
Scene 1: Wally notices a line of ants marching through the grass. "Where are they going?" he wonders.
Scene 2: Alice the Ant pops up. "We’re carrying food to our home!" she explains. Wally watches curiously.
Scene 3: Alice lifts a crumb with her tiny legs. "You’re so strong!" Wally says, amazed.
Scene 4: Wally follows the ant trail to their anthill. "This is where we live," Alice says proudly.
Scene 5: "I learned how hardworking ants are!" Wally says, waving goodbye to his tiny friends.
