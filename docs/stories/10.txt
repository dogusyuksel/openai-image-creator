212. Wally and the Iguanodon’s Thumb Spike
Scene 1: Wally spots a dinosaur eating low plants. "Your thumb looks sharp!"
Scene 2: Ivy the Iguanodon smiles. "It’s my thumb spike. I use it to protect myself."
Scene 3: Ivy demonstrates by poking a branch. "It’s helpful for defense."
Scene 4: Wally tries poking a leaf with his paw. "I guess I need sharper claws!"
Scene 5: "Thumb spikes are cool!" Wally says as Ivy continues eating peacefully.
