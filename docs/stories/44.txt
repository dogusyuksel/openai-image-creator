154. Wally and the Marmot's Tunnel
Scene 1: Wally hears whistling near some rocks. "Who’s whistling at me?" he wonders.
Scene 2: Marty the Marmot peeks out. "It’s me! I live underground."
Scene 3: Marty shows Wally his tunnel entrance. "This keeps me safe in winter!" Marty explains.
Scene 4: Wally tries crawling inside but gets stuck. "I need a bigger tunnel!" Wally laughs.
Scene 5: "Come visit when spring comes!" Marty says, waving as Wally heads down the mountain.
