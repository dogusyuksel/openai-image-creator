188. Wally and the Snowstorm Walk
Scene 1: Snow starts falling heavily. "I can’t see ahead!" Wally says.
Scene 2: Benny the Caribou appears. "Walk behind me! My fur keeps snow out."
Scene 3: Wally follows closely, watching Benny’s large hooves leave tracks.
Scene 4: "Your hooves stop you from sinking!" Wally says, impressed.
Scene 5: "Stick with me, and we’ll get through the storm!" Benny says confidently.
