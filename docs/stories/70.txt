99. Wally and the Racing Seahorses
Scene 1: Wally watches tiny seahorses bob in the waves. "You’re so small!" he says.
Scene 2: Sammy the Seahorse smiles. "We may be small, but we’re fast!" he says.
Scene 3: Wally watches as seahorses race through seaweed. "Go, Sammy!" he cheers.
Scene 4: "We hold onto things with our tails," Sammy explains, wrapping his tail around coral.
Scene 5: Wally claps. "You’re incredible, Sammy!" Sammy grins. "Thanks for cheering me on!"
