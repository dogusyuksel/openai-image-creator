224. Wally and the Space Puzzle Planet
Scene 1: Wally lands on a planet made of puzzle pieces. "This planet is strange!"
Scene 2: Piper the Puzzle Alien hops over. "Help me put it back together!"
Scene 3: Wally fits glowing pieces into place. "I love puzzles!"
Scene 4: The planet lights up. "It’s fixed!" Piper cheers.
Scene 5: "I’ll visit again for more puzzles!" Wally says happily.
