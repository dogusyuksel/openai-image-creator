95. Wally and the Jellyfish Parade
Scene 1: Wally watches glowing shapes drift near the shore. "What are those?" he asks curiously.
Scene 2: Jenny the Jellyfish floats by. "We’re jellyfish! We drift with the ocean currents."
Scene 3: Wally watches the jellyfish light up under the moon. "You’re like floating lanterns!" he says.
Scene 4: Jenny explains, "We glow to warn others. But don’t touch—our tentacles can sting!"
Scene 5: Wally waves. "I’ll enjoy watching from here!" Jenny glows brighter. "Thank you for understanding, Wally!"
