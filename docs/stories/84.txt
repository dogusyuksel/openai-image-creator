203. Wally and the Baby T-Rex
Scene 1: Wally hears a loud roar. "What was that?" he asks nervously.
Scene 2: Timmy the Baby T-Rex giggles. "It’s just me! I’m practicing roaring."
Scene 3: Wally joins Timmy, but his howl is quieter. "I need to practice too!"
Scene 4: Timmy grins. "Roaring makes us feel strong and brave!"
Scene 5: "Let’s roar together next time!" Wally says as they practice again.
