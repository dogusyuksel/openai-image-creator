113. Wally and the Lazy Sloth
Scene 1: Wally spots something moving very slowly along a branch. "Who’s that?" he asks.
Scene 2: Simon the Sloth waves. "I like to take things slow," Simon yawns.
Scene 3: Wally sits with Simon. "Why don’t you hurry?" Wally wonders. "Slowing down helps me enjoy the day," Simon explains.
Scene 4: Wally tries to move slowly too. "This is relaxing!" he says.
Scene 5: As Simon naps, Wally whispers, "I’ll take it slow sometimes too!" and tiptoes away quietly.
