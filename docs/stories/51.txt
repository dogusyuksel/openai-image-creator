152. Wally and the Eagle's Flight
Scene 1: Wally sees a shadow soaring over the cliffs. "Who’s flying up there?" he asks.
Scene 2: Ella the Eagle swoops down. "I’m Ella! I fly to watch over the mountains."
Scene 3: Ella spreads her wings. "Want to feel the wind?" Wally stands on a ledge, feeling the breeze.
Scene 4: Ella dives to catch her food. "Wow! You’re fast!" Wally says.
Scene 5: "Come back and watch me anytime!" Ella calls as she soars into the sky.
