218. Wally and the Mars Rovers
Scene 1: Wally steps onto Mars. "It’s so red and dusty here!"
Scene 2: Robbie the Rover rolls up. "I explore Mars to learn new things!"
Scene 3: Robbie shows Wally how to collect rock samples. "These might tell us about Mars’ past."
Scene 4: Wally places rocks in a box. "I’m helping with science!"
Scene 5: "Exploring is fun!" Wally says as Robbie drives into the distance.
