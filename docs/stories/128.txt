244. Wally and the Crocodile’s Smile
Scene 1: Wally spots a crocodile sunbathing. "Why are you lying there?"
Scene 2: Carl the Crocodile grins. "The sun keeps me warm."
Scene 3: Carl swims silently. "We stay cool by staying in the water too."
Scene 4: Wally copies him. "Sun and water, that’s the best of both!"
Scene 5: "I learned a lot today!" Wally says, waving to Carl.
