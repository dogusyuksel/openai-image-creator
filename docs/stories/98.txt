173. Wally and the Jackrabbit Race
Scene 1: Wally sees a rabbit hop quickly across the sand. "Wow, you’re fast!" Wally calls.
Scene 2: Jack the Jackrabbit grins. "I run fast to stay cool and escape predators."
Scene 3: Wally races Jack but can’t keep up. "You’re too quick!" Wally laughs.
Scene 4: Jack explains. "My big ears help me stay cool too!"
Scene 5: "I’ll keep practicing running!" Wally says, panting in the shade.
