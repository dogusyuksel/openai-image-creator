93. Wally and the Starfish Surprise
Scene 1: Wally explores tide pools at low tide. "Look at all the little creatures!" he says excitedly.
Scene 2: Sally the Starfish clings to a rock. "Hello, Wally!" Sally greets him. "I stick to rocks so I don’t float away!"
Scene 3: Wally gently touches Sally’s arm. "You feel soft!" he says. "We grow back if we lose an arm!" Sally explains.
Scene 4: Wally carefully places Sally back in the water. "I’ll visit you again!" he says.
Scene 5: As the tide rises, Wally waves. "Goodbye, Sally!" She waves back by wiggling her arms.
