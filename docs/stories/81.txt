134. Wally and the Elephant Parade
Scene 1: Wally sees a large shadow moving slowly. "Wow! You’re huge!" he says to Ellie the Elephant.
Scene 2: Ellie uses her trunk to pick leaves. "My trunk helps me eat and drink!" she explains.
Scene 3: Wally watches baby elephants following their mothers. "We stay together to protect each other," Ellie says.
Scene 4: Wally gets a ride on Ellie’s back. "This is so high up!" he laughs.
Scene 5: "Come back for another ride soon!" Ellie trumpets, as Wally waves from the ground.
