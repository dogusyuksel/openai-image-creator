208. Wally and the Diplodocus Tail Swing
Scene 1: Wally spots a dinosaur with a long tail. "That tail must be heavy!" he says.
Scene 2: Dylan the Diplodocus waves his tail. "It helps me stay balanced and keeps predators away."
Scene 3: Wally tries to swing his tail but wobbles. "I need a longer tail for balance!"
Scene 4: Dylan laughs. "My tail grows with me. One day, yours will too!"
Scene 5: "Your tail is amazing!" Wally says as Dylan swings it gracefully.
