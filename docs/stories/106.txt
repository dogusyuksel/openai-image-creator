94. Wally and the Sea Turtles’ Journey
Scene 1: Wally spots tiny turtles crawling toward the ocean. "Where are they going?" he wonders.
Scene 2: Tina the Turtle hatches nearby. "We’re heading to the sea! Can you help me?" she asks.
Scene 3: Wally gently guides Tina to the water. "You’re almost there!" he encourages.
Scene 4: Waves carry the baby turtles out to sea. "Good luck, Tina!" Wally shouts as they disappear into the water.
Scene 5: "Helping turtles feels great!" Wally says, watching the sunset.
