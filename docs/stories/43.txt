69. Wally and the Family of Deer
Scene 1: Wally walks quietly through the forest and spots a family of deer grazing. "Look at their tall antlers!" Wally whispers.
Scene 2: Danny the Deer notices Wally. "Come closer, but quietly," Danny says. Wally tiptoes gently.
Scene 3: Danny’s little brother jumps playfully. "We love running through the forest!" Danny explains.
Scene 4: Wally watches as the deer leap gracefully over fallen logs. "I wish I could jump like that!" he says.
Scene 5: Danny smiles. "Keep practicing, Wally!" Wally tries a small jump. "I’ll get better!" he laughs.
