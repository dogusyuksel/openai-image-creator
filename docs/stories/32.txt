170. Wally and the Sandstorm Shelter
Scene 1: The wind picks up, and sand swirls around Wally. "I can’t see!" he says.
Scene 2: Sami the Fennec Fox waves. "Follow me to my burrow!" he shouts.
Scene 3: Wally squeezes into Sami’s burrow. "This is cozy!" he says.
Scene 4: "We dig burrows to stay safe from sandstorms," Sami explains.
Scene 5: When the storm clears, Wally thanks Sami. "Burrows are great for hiding!"
