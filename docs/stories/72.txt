155. Wally and the Lynx's Hide-and-Seek
Scene 1: Wally spots large paw prints. "Who could make prints this big?" he asks.
Scene 2: Lana the Lynx appears from behind a tree. "I’m good at hiding!" she says.
Scene 3: Lana crouches low, blending with the rocks. Wally tries to find her but struggles.
Scene 4: "Found you!" Wally says excitedly. Lana smiles. "Let’s play again sometime!"
Scene 5: Wally follows Lana’s footprints back down, practicing his own hiding skills.
