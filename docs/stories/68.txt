112. Wally and the Parrot's Song
Scene 1: Wally hears loud squawking in the trees. "Who’s making that noise?" he wonders.
Scene 2: Penny the Parrot flies down. "I was singing! Want to learn a song, Wally?" Penny asks.
Scene 3: Penny sings a cheerful tune, and Wally repeats it. "Squawk! I sound like you!" Wally giggles.
Scene 4: Penny teaches Wally to mimic rainforest sounds, like dripping water and rustling leaves.
Scene 5: "You’re a great teacher, Penny!" Wally says, as they sing together, filling the rainforest with music.
