171. Wally and the Scarab Beetle
Scene 1: Wally sees a beetle rolling a ball of dirt. "What are you doing?" Wally asks.
Scene 2: Benny the Scarab Beetle grins. "I’m rolling food for later!"
Scene 3: Wally watches Benny push the ball uphill. "That looks hard!" Wally says.
Scene 4: Benny explains. "I store food underground to keep it fresh."
Scene 5: "You’re a great builder, Benny!" Wally says, as Benny rolls away.
