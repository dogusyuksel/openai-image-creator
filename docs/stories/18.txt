168. Wally and the Rattlesnake's Rattle
Scene 1: Wally hears a soft rattle behind him. "What’s that sound?" he wonders.
Scene 2: Robbie the Rattlesnake slithers out. "That’s me! My rattle warns others."
Scene 3: Wally watches Robbie shake his tail. "It’s loud!" Wally says, surprised.
Scene 4: Robbie laughs. "It helps me stay safe by telling others to stay back."
Scene 5: "I’ll remember to listen for rattles!" Wally says as Robbie disappears into the sand.
