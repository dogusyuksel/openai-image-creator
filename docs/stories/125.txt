96. Wally and the Curious Clownfish
Scene 1: Wally dips his paws into the water and sees something orange swimming by. "Who’s that?" he asks.
Scene 2: Colin the Clownfish peeks out of an anemone. "I live here! The anemone protects me," Colin explains.
Scene 3: Wally watches carefully. "Isn’t it dangerous?" Colin shakes his head. "Not for me, but it is for others!"
Scene 4: Wally watches Colin swim in and out, playing hide and seek. "You’re good at hiding!" Wally laughs.
Scene 5: "Come visit again!" Colin says. "I will, Colin!" Wally replies, happy to have a new ocean friend.
