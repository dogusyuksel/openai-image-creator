114. Wally and the Curious Chameleon
Scene 1: Wally notices a green leaf moving. "That leaf has legs!" he says, confused.
Scene 2: Charlie the Chameleon appears. "I can change colors to hide!" Charlie says, shifting to bright red.
Scene 3: Wally watches as Charlie blends into different plants. "You’re like magic!" Wally says.
Scene 4: Charlie shows Wally how he catches bugs with his long tongue. "That’s so fast!" Wally exclaims.
Scene 5: "You’re amazing, Charlie!" Wally says as Charlie disappears into the trees.
