166. Wally and the Camel Caravan
Scene 1: Wally walks across the hot sand, panting. "It’s so dry here!" he says.
Scene 2: Caleb the Camel approaches. "Need a ride? I can carry heavy loads!" Caleb offers.
Scene 3: Wally hops onto Caleb’s back. "Your hump is soft!" he says, holding on tightly.
Scene 4: Caleb explains. "My hump stores fat, so I can walk far without food!"
Scene 5: Wally waves from atop Caleb. "I love desert rides!" he says as the sun sets.
