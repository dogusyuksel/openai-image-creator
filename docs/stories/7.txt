185. Wally and the Northern Lights
Scene 1: Wally looks up at the sky. "The stars look different tonight!" he says.
Scene 2: Nora the Narwhal swims by. "That’s the Northern Lights! Watch closely!"
Scene 3: Wally watches as green and purple lights dance across the sky. "It’s magical!"
Scene 4: "We say it’s a gift from the sky," Nora whispers.
Scene 5: Wally watches until the lights fade. "I’ll never forget this night!"
