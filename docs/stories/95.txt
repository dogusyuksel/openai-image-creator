116. Wally and the Jungle River Adventure
Scene 1: Wally walks along the riverbank. "The water is so clear!" he says, watching fish swim.
Scene 2: Carla the Caiman floats by. "The river is my home," Carla says.
Scene 3: Wally steps into the shallow water. "It feels cool!" he says. Carla smiles. "Be careful! Some parts are deep."
Scene 4: Wally watches turtles sunbathe on rocks. "The river is full of life!" he says.
Scene 5: Carla guides Wally back to shore. "Thanks for showing me your home, Carla!" he says happily.
