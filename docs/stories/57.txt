132. Wally and the Laughing Hyenas
Scene 1: Wally hears giggling in the distance. "Who’s laughing so much?" he wonders.
Scene 2: Hazel the Hyena pops out of the grass. "We laugh to talk to each other!" she explains.
Scene 3: Wally tries to mimic their laugh. "Ha ha ha!" he says, making the hyenas giggle even more.
Scene 4: Hazel playfully chases Wally around. "Laughing makes everything fun!" she says.
Scene 5: "I’ll practice my laugh for next time!" Wally chuckles, as the hyenas disappear into the grass.
