211. Wally and the Troodon’s Night Hike
Scene 1: Wally walks through the forest at dusk. "It’s getting dark!" he says.
Scene 2: Toby the Troodon appears. "We hunt at night. I can see in the dark!"
Scene 3: Toby shows Wally how to listen for small animals. "Your ears help too!"
Scene 4: Wally tries it. "I can hear rustling!" he whispers.
Scene 5: "Night adventures are exciting!" Wally says as stars twinkle above.
