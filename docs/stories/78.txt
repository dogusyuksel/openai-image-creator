239. Wally and the Giraffe’s Tall Snack
Scene 1: Wally spots a giraffe munching on high leaves. "How do you reach those?"
Scene 2: Gina the Giraffe lowers her neck. "My long neck helps me!"
Scene 3: Wally stretches but can’t reach the leaves. "I need longer legs!"
Scene 4: Gina pulls down some leaves for Wally. "Here, try these!"
Scene 5: "Thank you, Gina! Friends help each other!" Wally says happily.
