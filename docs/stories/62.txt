98. Wally and the Singing Whales
Scene 1: Wally sits on a boat, listening to deep sounds in the distance. "What’s that noise?" he asks.
Scene 2: Wendy the Whale surfaces. "That’s our song! Whales sing to talk to each other."
Scene 3: Wally listens carefully. "It sounds peaceful," he says softly. Wendy hums a tune.
Scene 4: "We sing to find our way in the ocean," Wendy explains. Wally sings back playfully.
Scene 5: "You have a beautiful voice, Wally!" Wendy says. "I’ll keep practicing!" he giggles.
