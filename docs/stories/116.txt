156. Wally and the Crystal Cave
Scene 1: Wally finds a dark opening in the rocks. "I wonder what’s inside," he says.
Scene 2: Inside, sparkling crystals glow. "It’s like a treasure!" Wally whispers.
Scene 3: Cody the Bat flutters down. "This is my home! The crystals keep it bright."
Scene 4: Wally carefully steps around the crystals. "I love your home, Cody!"
Scene 5: "Come back at night, and I’ll show you the stars too!" Cody says as Wally exits.
