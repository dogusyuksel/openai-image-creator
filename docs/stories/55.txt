382. Wally and the Firefly Lanterns
Scene 1: Wally spots fireflies dancing outside. "They’re glowing like little lanterns!"
Scene 2: Mama Wolf nods. "They light the way for sleepy animals."
Scene 3: Wally imagines following the fireflies. "I’ll follow them to dreamland."
Scene 4: The fireflies guide him into soft, glowing dreams.
Scene 5: "Goodnight, fireflies," Wally whispers as he snuggles into bed.
