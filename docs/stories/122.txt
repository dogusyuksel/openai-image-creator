213. Wally and the Mosasaurus Splash
Scene 1: Wally sees splashing in the ocean. "Something big is swimming out there!"
Scene 2: Max the Mosasaurus surfaces. "I’m a sea reptile! Want to see how fast I swim?"
Scene 3: Max dives deep and surfaces again, creating a big wave.
Scene 4: Wally gets soaked. "You’re like a giant fish!"
Scene 5: "Come swim with me anytime!" Max says as he glides away.
