75. Wally and the Wise Old Tortoise
Scene 1: Wally spots Timothy the Tortoise slowly crossing a path. "Why are you moving so slowly?" Wally asks.
Scene 2: Timothy chuckles. "I like to enjoy the journey. Slow and steady wins the race!"
Scene 3: Wally walks alongside Timothy, matching his pace. "I can slow down too," Wally says.
Scene 4: Timothy tells Wally stories about the forest as they walk. "You know so much!" Wally says.
Scene 5: "Thank you for your wisdom, Timothy," Wally says. Timothy nods. "Patience is a great gift, Wally."
