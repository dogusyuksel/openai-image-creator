74. Wally and the Chattering Chipmunks
Scene 1: Wally sits by an oak tree and hears chattering above. "Who’s making that noise?" he asks.
Scene 2: Charlie the Chipmunk peeks down. "We’re collecting acorns!" he says, stuffing one into his cheeks.
Scene 3: Wally watches as chipmunks scurry around. "Your cheeks are so full!" he laughs.
Scene 4: Charlie shows Wally his hidden stash. "We store acorns to eat later," he explains.
Scene 5: "That’s clever!" Wally says. "I’ll come help next time!" Charlie nods happily.
