138. Wally and the Bouncy Springboks
Scene 1: Wally sees animals leaping high in the air. "They bounce like balls!" he says.
Scene 2: Sam the Springbok lands beside him. "We jump to show we’re healthy and strong!"
Scene 3: Wally tries jumping but can’t go as high. "I need stronger legs!" he laughs.
Scene 4: Sam teaches Wally to hop. "Keep practicing and you’ll bounce higher!"
Scene 5: "See you at the next jumping contest!" Sam smiles as he bounces away.
