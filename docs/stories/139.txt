73. Wally and the Fox Cubs
Scene 1: Wally hears playful giggling near a fallen tree. "Who’s there?" he asks.
Scene 2: Fiona the Fox appears with her cubs. "We’re playing hide and seek!" Fiona explains.
Scene 3: "Can I join?" Wally asks. The cubs nod excitedly. Wally hides behind a tree while the cubs count.
Scene 4: One by one, the cubs find Wally. "You’re good at hiding!" they giggle.
Scene 5: "Let’s play again soon!" Wally says. Fiona smiles. "You’re always welcome in the forest, Wally."
