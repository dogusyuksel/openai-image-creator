131. Wally and the Tall Giraffe
Scene 1: Wally walks through the savannah, shading his eyes from the sun. "The grass here is so tall!" he says.
Scene 2: Gloria the Giraffe bends down. "Hello down there! I’m Gloria!" she says, her long neck towering over Wally.
Scene 3: "How do you eat from so high up?" Wally asks. Gloria reaches for leaves at the top of a tree. "Like this!" she smiles.
Scene 4: Wally tries to jump but can’t reach. "I’ll stick to ground snacks!" he laughs.
Scene 5: "Let me know if you need a lift!" Gloria giggles, as Wally waves goodbye.
