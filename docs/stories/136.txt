199. Wally and the Curious Velociraptor
Scene 1: Wally hears rustling in the bushes. "Who’s there?" he asks.
Scene 2: Vinny the Velociraptor peeks out. "I’m exploring for food!"
Scene 3: Wally follows Vinny, watching him sniff the ground. "You’re fast and sneaky!"
Scene 4: Vinny grins. "I work with my friends to catch food."
Scene 5: "Teamwork makes you strong!" Wally says, excited to meet more raptors.
