175. Wally and the Mirage
Scene 1: Wally sees water in the distance. "I’m so thirsty!" he says, running toward it.
Scene 2: When Wally arrives, the water disappears. "Where did it go?" he asks.
Scene 3: Mila the Meerkat giggles. "That’s a mirage! The heat tricks your eyes."
Scene 4: Wally laughs. "I thought I found a lake!"
Scene 5: "Let’s find real water," Mila says, leading Wally to an oasis.

# Arctic Adventures (Stories 181-195)
Wally the Baby Wolf now explores icy Arctic landscapes, meeting animals that thrive in cold environments. These stories focus on resilience, teamwork, and survival in freezing temperatures.
