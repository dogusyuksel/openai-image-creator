201. Wally and the Ankylosaurus’s Armor
Scene 1: Wally sees a dinosaur covered in thick plates. "You look like a tank!"
Scene 2: Andy the Ankylosaurus chuckles. "My armor keeps me safe from predators."
Scene 3: Andy swings his club tail. "I use this to defend myself."
Scene 4: Wally gasps. "I’d love a tail like that!"
Scene 5: "Armor and tails make us tough!" Andy says, as Wally admires his strength.
