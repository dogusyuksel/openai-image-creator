157. Wally and the Icicle Forest
Scene 1: Icicles hang from branches as Wally walks through a frozen forest. "It’s like a glass forest!" he says.
Scene 2: Wendy the Weasel slides by. "I love sliding on ice!" Wendy laughs.
Scene 3: Wally tries sliding too but wobbles. "It’s harder than it looks!" he giggles.
Scene 4: Wendy shows Wally how to balance. "Keep your paws steady!"
Scene 5: "I’ll practice for next winter!" Wally says as Wendy slides away.
