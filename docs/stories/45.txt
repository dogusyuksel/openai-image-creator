117. Wally and the Fireflies at Night
Scene 1: As the rainforest gets darker, Wally sees tiny lights blinking. "What’s glowing over there?" he asks.
Scene 2: Freddie the Firefly lands on Wally’s nose. "We glow at night to find each other!" Freddie says.
Scene 3: Wally follows Freddie, watching fireflies light up the forest. "It’s like stars on the ground!" Wally says.
Scene 4: Freddie shows Wally how to gently cup his hands to catch and release the fireflies.
Scene 5: "Thanks for the light show, Freddie!" Wally says as the fireflies dance around him.
