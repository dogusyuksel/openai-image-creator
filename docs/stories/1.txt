186. Wally and the Seals’ Icy Game
Scene 1: Wally sees seals sliding on ice. "Can I play too?" he asks.
Scene 2: Sally the Seal claps her flippers. "Slide with us!" she says.
Scene 3: Wally races Sally to the water's edge but slips. "You’re faster on ice!" he laughs.
Scene 4: Sally explains. "Seals have smooth bodies for gliding!"
Scene 5: "Sliding is my new favorite game!" Wally says, as they slide again.
