97. Wally and the Friendly Octopus
Scene 1: Wally notices something change colors in the water. "What was that?" he wonders.
Scene 2: Olivia the Octopus appears. "I can change colors to hide!" she explains, turning bright purple.
Scene 3: Wally watches as Olivia squirts ink. "That’s to scare away anyone who bothers me," she says.
Scene 4: Olivia uses her tentacles to gather shells. "I’m very clever with my arms!" she smiles.
Scene 5: "You’re so smart, Olivia!" Wally says. "I’d love to learn more next time!" Olivia waves with a tentacle.
