366. Wally and the Baby Bird
Scene 1: Wally hears chirping near a bush. "What’s that sound?"
Scene 2: He spots a tiny baby bird. "Oh no! Are you okay?"
Scene 3: Mama Wolf says, "Let’s be gentle and careful."
Scene 4: Wally softly places leaves around the bird to keep it warm.
Scene 5: "I’ll take care of you until your mom comes back!" Wally whispers.
