222. Wally and the Milky Way Ride
Scene 1: Wally sees a glowing band of light. "What is that?"
Scene 2: Milo the Space Owl appears. "That’s the Milky Way, our galaxy!"
Scene 3: Milo invites Wally to glide along the stars. "Hold on tight!"
Scene 4: Wally flies through stars and comets. "It’s like a river of light!"
Scene 5: "The galaxy is full of magic!" Wally says, amazed.
