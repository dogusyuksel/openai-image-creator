119. Wally and the Jungle Frogs
Scene 1: Wally hears croaking from the plants. "Who’s making that sound?" he asks.
Scene 2: Felix the Frog hops over. "That’s us singing!" Felix says. "We love rainy days."
Scene 3: Wally watches as frogs leap from leaf to leaf. "You’re such great jumpers!" he says.
Scene 4: Felix shows Wally how to catch raindrops on his tongue. "Got one!" Wally laughs.
Scene 5: "I’ll visit again next time it rains!" Wally says, waving to Felix and his friends.
