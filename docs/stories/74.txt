241. Wally and the Dolphins’ Dance
Scene 1: Wally visits the ocean and sees dolphins jumping. "That looks fun!"
Scene 2: Danny the Dolphin splashes. "We love to play and swim together!"
Scene 3: Wally paddles in the water. "I’ll try swimming too!"
Scene 4: Danny shows Wally how to jump. "It’s all about practice!"
Scene 5: "Swimming is the best!" Wally says, splashing alongside his new friend.
