381. Wally and the Dreamy Train
Scene 1: "I can’t sleep!" Wally sighs.
Scene 2: Mama Wolf whispers. "Close your eyes and imagine a soft train ride."
Scene 3: Wally pictures a slow train chugging through the stars. "Choo choo… so quiet."
Scene 4: The train glides gently over the clouds. "It’s like floating!"
Scene 5: As the train rocks softly, Wally drifts off into sleep.
