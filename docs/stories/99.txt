204. Wally and the Dino Egg Surprise
Scene 1: Wally finds large eggs in a nest. "Whose eggs are these?"
Scene 2: Dora the Dinosaur waddles up. "They’re mine! I’m waiting for them to hatch."
Scene 3: Wally watches as the eggs wiggle. "They’re moving!"
Scene 4: "The babies are almost ready!" Dora says proudly.
Scene 5: "I can’t wait to meet them!" Wally says, excited to return.
