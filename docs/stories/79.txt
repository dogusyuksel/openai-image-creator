378. Wally’s Dreamy Cloud Ride
Scene 1: "I wish I could sleep on a cloud," Wally yawns.
Scene 2: Mama Wolf tells a story. "Close your eyes and imagine floating on one."
Scene 3: Wally imagines drifting high above the trees. "It’s so soft up here!"
Scene 4: He giggles, bouncing gently on the fluffy cloud.
Scene 5: "I’ll visit the clouds every night in my dreams."
