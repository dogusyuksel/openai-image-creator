197. Wally and the Playful Triceratops
Scene 1: Wally hears stomping and sees three-horned dinosaurs running. "What are they doing?"
Scene 2: Trixie the Triceratops smiles. "We’re playing chase! Want to join?"
Scene 3: Wally races with Trixie but gets left behind. "You’re fast!" Wally huffs.
Scene 4: Trixie explains. "My strong legs help me run from danger."
Scene 5: "Let’s play again soon!" Wally says as Trixie waves goodbye.
