207. Wally and the Gallimimus Race
Scene 1: Wally hears stomping. "What’s that sound?" he wonders.
Scene 2: Grace the Gallimimus speeds by. "We’re racing! Join us!" she shouts.
Scene 3: Wally runs alongside but struggles to keep up. "You’re so fast!"
Scene 4: Grace explains. "We run to escape predators. Our legs are strong and quick!"
Scene 5: Wally cheers as Grace wins. "I’ll keep practicing my running!"
