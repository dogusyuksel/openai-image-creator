216. Wally’s Rocket Ride
Scene 1: Wally finds a shiny rocket in the forest. "What’s this?!" he asks, pressing buttons.
Scene 2: The rocket’s door opens. "Let’s go to space!" a voice says.
Scene 3: Wally climbs inside. "I’m ready!" The rocket launches into the sky.
Scene 4: Wally looks out the window. "I can see all of Earth!"
Scene 5: "Space is amazing!" Wally says as stars twinkle around him.
