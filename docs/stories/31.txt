386. Wally and the Cloudy Blanket
Scene 1: "I want to sleep under a cloud," Wally yawns.
Scene 2: Mama Wolf tucks his blanket tighter. "Pretend your blanket is a cloud."
Scene 3: Wally snuggles deeper. "It feels soft like one!"
Scene 4: He drifts off, imagining fluffy clouds surrounding him.
Scene 5: "Goodnight, cloud blanket," he mumbles as he falls asleep.
