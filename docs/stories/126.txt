174. Wally and the Desert Owl
Scene 1: The sun sets, and Wally hears hooting. "Who’s out at night?" he wonders.
Scene 2: Olivia the Owl lands nearby. "I hunt at night when it’s cooler!"
Scene 3: Olivia flies silently. "I have soft feathers to stay quiet," she explains.
Scene 4: Wally listens to Olivia’s hoots, feeling calm.
Scene 5: "Nighttime is peaceful," Wally says, curling up to sleep.
