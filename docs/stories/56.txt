242. Wally and the Clever Owl
Scene 1: Wally hears hooting at night. "Who’s there?"
Scene 2: Olivia the Owl perches on a branch. "I stay awake to watch over the forest."
Scene 3: Wally listens as Olivia explains how owls see in the dark. "That’s amazing!"
Scene 4: Olivia spots a mouse below. "I can see far and wide!"
Scene 5: "I’d love to have owl eyes!" Wally says as Olivia flies away.
