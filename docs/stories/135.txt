167. Wally and the Lizard's Sunbath
Scene 1: Wally spots something small and shiny resting on a rock. "What are you doing up there?" Wally asks.
Scene 2: Leo the Lizard blinks. "I’m warming up in the sun! It keeps me strong."
Scene 3: Wally tries sunbathing but gets too hot. "How do you handle the heat?" he asks.
Scene 4: "I find shade when it’s too hot," Leo says, scurrying under a bush.
Scene 5: Wally joins Leo in the shade. "Sunbathing is fun, but shade feels better!"
