184. Wally and the Walrus Party
Scene 1: Wally hears loud splashing near the shore. "What’s making that noise?"
Scene 2: Walter the Walrus waves his flippers. "It’s me and my friends!" he bellows.
Scene 3: Walter shows Wally how walruses use their tusks to pull up onto the ice.
Scene 4: Wally tries but slides back. "You’re strong!" Wally says, impressed.
Scene 5: "Join our walrus party anytime!" Walter calls as Wally waves goodbye.
