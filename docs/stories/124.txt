225. Wally and the Black Hole Adventure
Scene 1: Wally sees a swirling black circle. "What is that?"
Scene 2: Barry the Black Hole chuckles. "I’m a black hole! I pull things in."
Scene 3: Barry gently lets Wally float nearby. "I won’t pull you in, don’t worry."
Scene 4: Wally spins slowly. "It’s like a cosmic whirlpool!"
Scene 5: "Space is full of mysteries!" Wally says, drifting back to his rocket.
