247. Wally Says Thank You
Scene 1: Wally’s friend gives him a shiny rock. "Wow, this is cool!"
Scene 2: His friend smiles but looks puzzled. "What should I say?" Wally wonders.

Scene 4: His friend beams. "You’re welcome!"
Scene 5: "Saying thank you feels good!" Wally decides to thank others more often.
