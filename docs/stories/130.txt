72. Wally and the Sleeping Bear
Scene 1: Wally stumbles upon a cave. "Hello?" he calls softly, hearing gentle snoring inside.
Scene 2: Benny the Bear stretches and yawns. "I’m getting ready for hibernation," Benny says sleepily.
Scene 3: Wally tilts his head. "What’s hibernation?" Benny explains, "I sleep through the winter to stay warm."
Scene 4: Wally helps Benny gather berries. "These will keep me full before I sleep," Benny says gratefully.
Scene 5: As Benny dozes off, Wally whispers, "Sweet dreams, Benny!" and tiptoes away quietly.
