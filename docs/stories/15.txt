243. Wally and the Marching Penguins
Scene 1: Wally visits a snowy land. "Who are these waddling birds?"
Scene 2: Penny the Penguin waddles up. "We march to stay warm!"
Scene 3: Wally marches beside them. "This is fun!"
Scene 4: Penny slides on the ice. "We slide too!"
Scene 5: "I love winter fun!" Wally says as they all slide down icy hills.
