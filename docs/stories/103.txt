169. Wally and the Cactus Flower
Scene 1: Wally sees a tall cactus covered in bright flowers. "How can flowers grow here?" he wonders.
Scene 2: Carrie the Cactus smiles. "I store water inside, even when it doesn’t rain!"
Scene 3: Wally tries to touch the cactus but jumps back. "Ouch! You’re spiky!" he says.
Scene 4: "My spines protect me from thirsty animals," Carrie explains.
Scene 5: Wally admires the flowers. "You’re beautiful and tough!" he says, waving goodbye.
