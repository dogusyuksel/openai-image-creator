205. Wally and the Woolly Mammoth Herd
Scene 1: Wally steps into a snowy field. "Is this still the dinosaur world?" he asks.
Scene 2: Molly the Woolly Mammoth stomps up. "Nope! We live in the Ice Age!"
Scene 3: Molly’s thick fur sways as she walks. "My fur keeps me warm."
Scene 4: Wally touches her fur. "It’s soft and thick!"
Scene 5: "Come visit the Ice Age anytime!" Molly says as her herd marches by.
