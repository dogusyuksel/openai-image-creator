189. Wally and the Penguin Parade
Scene 1: Wally sees a line of penguins waddling along. "Where are you all going?"
Scene 2: Penny the Penguin laughs. "We’re heading to our nest!"
Scene 3: Wally waddles with them. "Marching in a line is fun!"
Scene 4: Penny explains. "It keeps us safe and warm!"
Scene 5: "I’ll march with you anytime!" Wally says, hopping along with the parade.
