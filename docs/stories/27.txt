196. Wally and the Gentle Brontosaurus
Scene 1: Wally finds himself in a lush, green forest. "Everything looks so big!" he says.
Scene 2: Brenda the Brontosaurus munches on tall leaves. "I’m a plant-eater," she explains.
Scene 3: Wally tugs on a vine. "How do you reach such high leaves?" he asks.
Scene 4: Brenda stretches her long neck. "My neck helps me eat from tall trees!"
Scene 5: Wally giggles. "I’d need a ladder!" he jokes, watching Brenda eat peacefully.
