220. Wally and the Star Constellations
Scene 1: Wally floats in space, staring at bright stars. "They look like pictures!"
Scene 2: Stella the Starfish drifts by. "Those are constellations!"
Scene 3: Stella points. "That’s Orion the Hunter, and there’s the Big Dipper."
Scene 4: Wally connects stars with his paw. "I see them now!"
Scene 5: "I’ll draw constellations in the sky every night!" Wally smiles.
