137. Wally and the Ostrich Race
Scene 1: Wally spots large birds running fast. "I didn’t know birds could run so quickly!" he says.
Scene 2: Olivia the Ostrich stops beside him. "We’re the fastest birds in the savannah!"
Scene 3: Wally races Olivia, but she speeds ahead. "You’re faster than me!" he laughs.
Scene 4: Olivia flaps her wings. "Even though I can’t fly, I run fast to stay safe."
Scene 5: "I’ll train harder for next time!" Wally says as Olivia zooms away.
