245. Wally and the Fireflies’ Light Show
Scene 1: Wally walks through a meadow at dusk. "What are those glowing lights?"
Scene 2: Fiona the Firefly flies up. "We light up to talk to each other!"
Scene 3: Wally watches as the fireflies blink in patterns. "It’s like a star show!"
Scene 4: Fiona teaches Wally how to blink his eyes like fireflies.
Scene 5: "I’ll watch the fireflies every night!" Wally says, glowing with excitement.
