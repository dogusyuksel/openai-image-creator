209. Wally and the Baby Plesiosaur
Scene 1: Wally visits a prehistoric lake. "I wonder who lives here?"
Scene 2: Pearl the Baby Plesiosaur peeks out. "I do! Want to swim?"
Scene 3: Wally dips his paw in the water. "It’s chilly but fun!"
Scene 4: Pearl glides underwater. "My long neck helps me swim and look for food."
Scene 5: "I’d love to swim like you!" Wally says as Pearl shows him around the lake.
